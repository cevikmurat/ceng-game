How to Play
Three minutes to learn

Goal. Move all 8 of your pieces into the opponent's home zone, each onto its matching symbol slot. First to do so wins.

Turn. Roll the die. Move the matching shape one square in any direction.

Pass (P). Lose your turn.

Smiley. Roll again, then make two moves with your matching pieces (same piece twice or two different pieces) and one move on your opponent's matching piece. Roll smiley twice in a row for an even bigger turn.

Safe. A piece on its own symbol slot in the opponent's zone is safe — the opponent can't move it.

Cengo. Complete the full pattern in one turn to call cengo and earn 4 free moves.
